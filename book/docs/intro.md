# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics** textbook.

This book is designed to teach how artificial intelligence systems move beyond
digital environments into the physical world through embodiment.

You will learn:
- ROS 2 as the robotic nervous system
- Simulation-first development with Gazebo and Unity
- AI-powered perception and control with NVIDIA Isaac
- Vision–Language–Action systems for humanoid robots
