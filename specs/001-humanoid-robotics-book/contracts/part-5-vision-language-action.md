# Learning Objectives for Part 5: Vision–Language–Action Systems

## Chapter 9: Connecting LLMs to ROS 2
- The reader will be able to create a ROS 2 node that communicates with an LLM.
- The reader will be able to design prompts to elicit structured robotic commands from an LLM.

## Chapter 10: Building a Vision-Language-Action System
- The reader will be able to integrate a vision model into a ROS 2 system.
- The reader will be able to build an end-to-end VLA system that can respond to natural language commands.
