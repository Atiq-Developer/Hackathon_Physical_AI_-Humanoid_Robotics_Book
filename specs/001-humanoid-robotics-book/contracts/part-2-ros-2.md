# Learning Objectives for Part 2: The Robotic Nervous System (ROS 2)

## Chapter 3: ROS 2 Architecture
- The reader will be able to explain the ROS 2 graph and its core concepts.
- The reader will be able to use the ROS 2 command-line interface to inspect a running system.

## Chapter 4: Building a ROS 2 System
- The reader will be able to create a ROS 2 workspace and packages.
- The reader will be able to write a simple publisher and subscriber node in Python.
