# Learning Objectives for Part 1: Foundations

## Chapter 1: Introduction to Physical AI
- The reader will be able to define Physical AI and explain its significance.
- The reader will be able to describe the key challenges in humanoid robotics.

## Chapter 2: Embodied Intelligence
- The reader will be able to explain the embodiment thesis.
- The reader will be able to discuss how sensors and actuators influence a robot's intelligence.
