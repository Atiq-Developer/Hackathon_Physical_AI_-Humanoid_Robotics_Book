# Learning Objectives for Part 4: AI Perception, Learning, and Control

## Chapter 7: Perception with NVIDIA Isaac
- The reader will be able to set up and use NVIDIA Isaac Sim for perception tasks.
- The reader will be able to train a simple perception model using synthetic data.

## Chapter 8: Control Pipelines
- The reader will be able to explain the components of a robotic control loop.
- The reader will be able to use MoveIt 2 to plan and execute a simple motion.
